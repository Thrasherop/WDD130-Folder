Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this white water rafting site is to attract and inform potential customers about our business and rafting packages. This is so that we can increase profits and growth. We also plan to advertise our business on social media, and this site will provide the destination for customer clicks.

Audience

There are two types of visitors we expect: customers and potential business partners. We are targeting a customer base who likes adventures and outdoor activities. We expect customers who are between the ages of 16 and 30. This site will act as an avenue to inform and sell our products to these customers. We are also hoping to attract potential business partners (such as resorts looking to outsource rafting) to our business via this website.
