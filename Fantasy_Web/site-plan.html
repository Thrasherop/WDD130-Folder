Overview

Purpose

For over a year now, I’ve been running a Minecraft server for friends and family. We’ve had many fun worlds, all of which have unique memories. There is an issue, however: everyone wants access to these world files at random points in time, but the files are too massive to easily transfer them around. How can I make these Minecraft worlds easily distributable? A website. I would like to create a website that would have download links for said files. This would allow my loved ones to go to a URL and download the world files without having to contact me, thus having a reliable and frictionless way to easily access their memories. Additionally, I will be able to have a portfolio page to show potential employers my proudest projects.

Audience

The main audience for this website would be my fellow Minecraft players. They would be able to use this site to download prized worlds, which is something I've not been able to give them before. Another audience would be future employers. I intend to use this site itself as a passion project, as well as have a page to act as my portfolio. Lastly, a small portion of my visitors will be family and friends that I simply want to show my work to. I want this page to be something I'm truly proud of!
